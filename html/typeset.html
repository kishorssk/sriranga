Typesetting

In Sriranga we use TeX/LaTeX/XeLaTeX for typesetting books and Journals.

We have nearly 15 years of experience in producing books using these technologies.

We also have an experience in typesetting critical editions, it means books having multiple footnote

levels, for example, Amarakosha.

We have also written a package for typesetting Kannada books.

Prism Books Private Limited, Bengaluru

We have established a fruitful strategic association with Prism Books PvtLtd., Bangalore, a leading publishing house in India. We have typeset plenty of text books for PUC/BE classes which are already in the market. Anothermajor project for Prism Books is the Digitization of the four volume English-Kannada Dictionary of the University of Mysore which will be availablein the market in the form of a CD.

Society Mathematique de France (SMF): France

Another major association of our company is with Society Mathematiquede France (SMF), Paris, France, for whom we are digitizing and archivingtheir earlier publications. Typeset nearly 5000 pages of mathematics lecture course for SMF, France.

Alliance University:

Typesetting the journal Alliance Journal of Business Research and AAC since 2005.

University of Mysore: Mysuru

Typesetting mySCIENCE, a journal of University of Mysore and also a book on Dharmashastra Itihasa. Produced an electronic version of all the four volumes of English Kannada Dictionary. We have also typeset critical edition Amarakosha for Prasaranga Mysuru.

Indian Academy of Sciences:

Typeset Journal of Genetics since 2006. Indian Academy now trying to bring books by eminent authors containing various articles published in their journals. We are now typesetting those books and also producing e-books in epub format. These e-books can be downloaded freely from Academy website.

TIFRBombay

We have typeset nearly 100 books in "Lecture Notes Series" and "Studies-in-Mathematics" categories for TIFR

University of Tokyo: Japan

Written a style file for digitization of “Kannada-English-Japanese” dictionary for University of Tokyo, Japan.

Adhytmaprakashana Karyalaya, Holenarasipura

Typeset Kannada Grammer, Raghuvamsa and few other titles for this organization

Omkar Creations Bengaluru

Digitisation

Sriranga is a pioneer in the field of digital archiving of indic language material and artistic works,.
To the best of our knowledge, Sriranga is the only company in India who have been able to successfully produce digital archives of large sets of indic language documents and journals.

Web Development

Research in Indic language computing (OCR, Fonts)
